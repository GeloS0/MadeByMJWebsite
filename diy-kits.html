How it works

Four steps from box to backdrop.

01

Pick your kit

Choose your length (6 ft to 18 ft) and theme. Every price already includes the balloons, garland strip, glue dots, hooks, and your custom Cricut-cut sign or topper — what you see is what you pay.

02

We cut & pack it fresh

We cut your custom sign and pack your kit pre-sorted by size and color, with a written and video walkthrough. Pick it up locally or we'll deliver it in the Colorado Springs area — no shipping wait.

03

Assemble in under an hour

Clip the balloons onto the strip in the pattern shown in your guide, add glue dots for shape, and attach your custom sign. No helium, no special tools — just your hands and about 45–60 minutes.

04

Hang & celebrate

Use the included command hooks to hang your garland wherever your event calls for it. Indoors and out of direct sun, it holds its shape and color for 2–4 weeks.

Ask about a custom kit

Good to know

Kit questions, answered.

What makes a Made by MJ kit different from a big-box balloon kit?+

Big-box kits use thin, single-size balloons that deflate or discolor within a day, with no real design plan — just a bag of balloons and a strip of tape. Our kits use the same professional-grade, multi-size balloon assortments we use in our paid installations, arranged in a tested pattern so your garland looks intentionally styled, not lumpy — plus a custom Cricut sign no big-box kit includes.

Will my garland actually look like the photos?+

Yes — because we design the color and size ratios the same way we do for our in-person installs, then translate that exact recipe into your kit. You're not guessing at proportions; you're following our proven formula, step by step.

What's included in the price?+

Everything: all balloons for your chosen length, the balloon strip/garland tape, glue dots, command hooks, a custom Cricut-cut sign or topper in your theme, and free local pickup (or local delivery in the Colorado Springs area). There is nothing added at checkout.

How long does assembly take?+

Most customers finish in 45–60 minutes with no experience required. A step-by-step written and video guide is included with every kit.

How long will the garland last?+

Indoors and out of direct sun, your garland holds its shape and color for 2–4 weeks. Outdoor use in direct sun or heat above 90°F shortens that window, just as it does with any latex balloon decor.

Do you offer pickup or delivery?+

Both, locally. Free pickup in Colorado Springs, or choose local delivery within the Colorado Springs area — you'll pick your option at checkout. Kits are made fresh, so please allow a few days' lead time.

Can I customize the colors or the sign wording?+

Yes. Every kit's Cricut-cut sign can be personalized with a name, age, date, or short message at no extra charge — it's part of your flat price, not an add-on. Palettes can be recolored too; just note it on your order.

Do you ship kits outside Colorado Springs?+

Not yet — for now, kits are local Colorado Springs pickup and delivery only. Nationwide shipping is on our roadmap; text us if you'd like to be first to know when it launches.
